Self-Assessment

Regularly assess your gaming habits. Ask yourself if you're playing for entertainment or if gaming has become a problem in your life.

Set Limits

Establish time and spending limits before you start playing. Stick to these limits and never chase losses.

Take Breaks

Take regular breaks from gaming. Step away from the screen and engage in other activities to maintain balance.

Warning Signs

If you recognize any of these signs, you may need to seek help:

  • Spending more time or money than you can afford
  • Neglecting responsibilities or relationships
  • Lying about your gaming activities
  • Feeling anxious or depressed when not gaming
  • Chasing losses with bigger bets

Self-Exclusion Tools

We provide several tools to help you manage your gaming:

  • Deposit Limits: Set daily, weekly, or monthly deposit limits
